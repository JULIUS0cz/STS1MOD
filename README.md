All Slay the Spire 2 solo mode Ironclad cards have been mixed in with Slay the Spire 1 Ironclad cards. Now, selecting Ironclad allows you to obtain both Act 1 and Act 2 cards, and new versions of Perfected Strike and BleedLetting have been added.
You can use this mod alongside BaseMod and ModTheSpire after installing them for Slay the Spire 1.
